Server Rules

Dolphini SMP is a vanilla survival server built on trust, fairness, and respect. These rules exist to keep the experience enjoyable for everyone.

1. General Conduct

  • Be respectful to all players. Harassment, hate speech, or excessive toxicity is not allowed.
  • No impersonation of staff or other players.
  • Keep chat appropriate. Use common sense.

2. Cheating & Exploits

  • No hacked clients, cheats, or unfair mods (x-ray, kill aura, auto-clickers, etc.).
  • No exploiting bugs, glitches, or unintended mechanics.
  • Allowed mods include performance and visual mods (OptiFine, Sodium, minimaps without radar).

3. Griefing & Stealing

  • Griefing other players' builds is not allowed.
  • Stealing from player bases or chests is not allowed.
  • PvP does not give permission to grief or steal.

4. PvP Rules

  • PvP is allowed unless otherwise agreed upon.
  • No spawn camping or repeatedly targeting the same player.
  • Respect PvP opt-outs and community agreements.

5. Builds & World Respect

  • No offensive or inappropriate builds.
  • Do not intentionally lag the server (lag machines, excessive entities).
  • Large farms are allowed but may be limited if they cause performance issues.

6. Staff & Enforcement

  • Staff decisions are final. If you disagree, discuss it calmly.
  • Punishments scale based on severity and history.
  • Attempting to bypass a punishment will result in harsher penalties.
These rules may be updated as the server grows. By playing on Dolphini SMP, you agree to follow them.
